# Product — Cloudberry

**Cloudberry is a closed photographic archive.** The solar camera is gone; there is no planned return to continuous capture. The live experience’s job is to present that finite corpus well — gallery, viewer, about, maps, exhibition/kiosk surfaces — with durable citations and solid performance on Cloudflare.
